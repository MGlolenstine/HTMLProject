About the company

Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg.
Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month
long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes to empower themselves
and can provide more marketable skills.

What the website is about

You will be able to learn The SME is an initiative by Precious Radebe to provide skills training for domestic
workers and gardeners. Her parents and other elderly relatives were never given the
chance to upskill themselves or follow a formal educational qualification so this training
school is her way of supporting similarly affected members from her community.
The training she provides uplifts domestic workers and gardeners by making them more
marketable when seeking employment and enables them to be paid at a higher rate
because of their additional skills. They can also be entrepreneurs and setup their own
small business utilising these newly obtained skills. Many employers would like to have
their employees upskilled to offer more advanced services to the household.
